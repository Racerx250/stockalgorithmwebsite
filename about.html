What is Toetrekker?

A bot that handles all of my finances.

Whether it be stocks, crypto, or bonds, Toetrekker is a system I designed that trades and manages my portfolios autonomously without any need for my interference.

It's made up currently of several recurring scripts that my desktop computer runs on a 15 second to 1 day interval.


How do I use this?

Do whatever you want.

Copy my trades, short my positions, maybe even create a script yourself that rips my trades from this website. Or just stay and watch. The choice is yours.
